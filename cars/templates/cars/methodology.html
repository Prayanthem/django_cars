Collection

The data is gathered programmatically. Duplicates are erased, and values are cleaned.

Maintanence

The database does not undergo frequent maintanence as it is running on free hosting.

Limitations

Practical limitations

The data does not show real sale value. Estimations are all based on ask prices on a website where backroom bidding is allowed and encouraged. There is no information available in the dataset on actual selling price if a sale takes place. It is, however, a reasonable assumption that the cars are sold for less than the ask price. As such the face value price on estimation is probably overestimated. In the future, if data on price on sale is collected then that a bias can be corrected for.

The dataset is small, and may not be representative for the second-hand market.
